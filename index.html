Meetup & resources for those who teach coding

Join us for our next meetup: 17th September at Twitter in London.

Lots of coders teach. We run meetups for people learning, mentor newbies, or teach at bootcamps. But we don't often get together to talk about teaching.

Coders Who Teach is a space for sharing experience and best practice in teaching programming outside of the school classroom; whether at meetups, bootcamps or coding courses.
