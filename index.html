Teach

Meetup & resources for those who teach coding

Lots of coders teach - whether running meetups for learners, mentoring newbies, or teaching at bootcamps. But we don't often get together to talk about teaching. Coders Who Teach is a community focussed on how we can grow and improve as teachers, so we can provide the best for our students and mentees.

Coders Who Teach meetups are a space for sharing experience and best practice in teaching programming outside of the school classroom; whether at meetups, bootcamps or coding courses.
